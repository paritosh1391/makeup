Job Assistance

RVMUA does not guarantee job placement for graduates. However, we understand the difficulties when beginning a makeup career, which is why we help our students with the job-hunting process.

RVMUA maintains a makeup industry job board, which is continually updated with a wide range of employment opportunities in beauty makeup, fashion makeup, and Media makeup. In addition to posting these makeup jobs on campus, RVMUA staff sends out a “Daily Jobs” e-mail detailing the latest makeup opportunities available to our students and graduates.

We are well-known in the makeup and entertainment industry. We are approached by designers, production teams, and filmmakers looking to hire RVMUA graduates, exclusively submitted through our website.
