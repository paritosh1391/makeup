RVMUA Makeup Academy Scholarships

If you want to be a makeup artist or a hairstylist you are already thinking out of the box. A professional course in the field will take you places. Let not money aspects bother you because at RVMUA we offer regular Scholarships worth Rs 50,000/- each to two deserving candidates per batch.

RVMUA was founded by the celebrity professional makeup artist Riya Vashist to create a more comprehensive makeup artist academy that offers up-to-date knowledge to aspiring makeup and special effects technicians.

The Scholarship is awarded to deserving young candidates who demonstrate talent, drive, and passion.

Scholarship Award:

Each RVMUA Scholarship will consist of Rs 50,000/- scholarship award to be applied towards any RVMUA program.

The Scholarship will be awarded to two candidates per batch who show passion to get trained in the area of makeup artistry

Eligibility:

Each individual applying for the Scholarship must be over the age of 18, and must have a proof of high school completion or its equivalent.

Nationality:

Only Indian nationals are eligible to apply for this Scholarship program.

How to Apply:

The mode of application is online.
