About Us

Riya Vashist is highly regarded as one of the most popular names within the beauty, fashion & tv industry. Trained in SFX Prosthetic Makeup from Los Angeles, the United States of America, Riya has worked in the makeup business for over 13 years with her modern and natural approach having done countless high-profile celebrities, editors, photographers, producers and fashion designers alike.

Riya is also distinguished as an accomplished beauty expert, setting her apart from other industry make-up artists, Riya is now one of the most acclaimed international makeup artists and Being a leading celebrity makeup artist, Riya brings considerable experience and passion for beauty, and a willingness to dynamically adapt as a project evolves. Offering not just celebrity make up but also Bridal makeup, special occasion makeup.
