Airbrush Makeup Course (ABM)

Time Duration : Seven Days
₹ 50,000.00

We offer an intensive course in Airbrush Makeup that scores much higher than traditional makeup in terms of smooth and flawless skin texture. Our Airbrush makeup class covers the fundamentals of Airbrush Makeup application and techniques. Airbrushing is popular in Film, Fashion, TV, Theater, and Wedding but is most famous in HD (High Definition) work for its even and polished appearance.

Course Description

  • Understanding of Skin and Airbrush equipment
  • Product recommendations
  • Foundation and Blushers: How to mix colors to get right base and application of the base using Airbrush
  • Contouring and Highlighting
  • Application of Eye makeup using Airbrush
  • Creating Fashion look using the technique
  • Cleaning and maintaining the machine
